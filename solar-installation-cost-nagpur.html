System Size Recommendations for Nagpur Homes

1. Small Home (Monthly Bill: ₹1,500 - ₹3,000)

  • System Size: 2-3 kW
  • Total Cost: ₹1.0 - ₹1.95 lakh
  • Subsidy: ₹78,000 (for up to 2 kW) or ₹1.17 lakh (for 3 kW)
  • Net Cost: ₹22,000 - ₹1.17 lakh
  • Payback: 2.5-3.5 years (fastest in Maharashtra)

2. Medium Home (Monthly Bill: ₹3,000 - ₹6,000)

  • System Size: 4-5 kW
  • Total Cost: ₹2.0 - ₹3.25 lakh
  • Subsidy: ₹1.17 lakh (capped at 3 kW)
  • Net Cost: ₹83,000 - ₹2.08 lakh
  • Payback: 3-4 years

Special Considerations for Nagpur

Summer Performance

Extreme summers (45°C+) can reduce panel efficiency by 10-15%. Choose panels with high temperature coefficient.

Dust Management

Nagpur is dusty - plan for panel cleaning every 45 days. This affects output by 5-8% if ignored.

Electricity Tariff

MSEDCL tariffs in Nagpur region: ₹5.5-7.5/unit (lower than Mumbai but still viable for solar).
