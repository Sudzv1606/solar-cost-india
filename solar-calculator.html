Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate are these estimates?

Our calculator provides estimates within 10-15% of actual quotes. Final costs depend on site survey, installer choice, and specific components selected.

Is the subsidy guaranteed?

Central subsidy is available if you meet eligibility criteria. Final approval depends on MNRE verification and documentation. We show all requirements in results.

What's not included in the cost?

Estimates include complete system cost. Not included: any structural reinforcement costs, additional civil work, or battery backup (if needed).

How soon should I install?

Subsidy amounts may decrease as adoption increases. Current tariffs and subsidy structure make 2025 a good year for installation.
