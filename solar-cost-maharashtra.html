MSEDCL Net Metering Process (2025)

Step 1: Online Application

MSEDCL portal with:

  • Electricity bill copy
  • Property documents
  • Aadhaar/PAN card

Time: 2-3 days

Step 2: Load Calculation

MSEDCL reviews:

  • Sanctioned load
  • Historical consumption
  • Transformer capacity

Time: 5-7 days

Step 3: Technical Feasibility

Site inspection if required:

  • Road accessibility
  • Shadow study
  • Structural feasibility

Time: 7-10 days

Step 4: LOA Issuance

Letter of Approval:

  • Valid for 6 months
  • Specifies net meter type
  • Includes installation checklist

Time: 3-5 days

Step 5: Installation

Installer completes:

  • System installation
  • Inspection by MSEDCL
  • Test report submission

Time: 5-7 days

Step 6: Net Meter Installation

Bi-directional meter:

  • Installed by MSEDCL
  • Sealed and tested
  • Connected to grid

Time: 15-20 days

Total Timeline: 35-50 days (varies by city)
