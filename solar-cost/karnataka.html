Electricity tariffs & impact on savings

Residential electricity tariffs in Karnataka are structured with progressive slabs, making solar particularly attractive for moderate to high consumption households:

  • Higher tariff slabs lead to faster payback for solar
  • Time-of-day tariffs in some areas increase solar value
  • Net metering credits are settled monthly

DISCOMs in Karnataka have different tariff structures. BESCOM (Bangalore) typically has higher tariffs compared to other DISCOMs, which affects the financial viability of solar installations.

Typical payback period in Karnataka

5-7 years for properly sized systems, with Bangalore seeing faster payback due to higher tariffs

DISCOM process

Approvals handled by respective DISCOMs based on location:

  • BESCOM for Bangalore urban and rural
  • CESCOM for Mysore region
  • GESCOM for Gulbarga area
  • HESCOM for Hubballi region

Net metering policies are generally consistent across DISCOMs, with minor procedural variations.
