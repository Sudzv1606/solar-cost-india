Solar Installation Cost in Mumbai (2025)

Mumbai presents unique challenges for solar adoption with India's highest installation costs, extreme space constraints, and complex urban infrastructure. However, the city's astronomical electricity tariffs and limited alternatives make solar an increasingly attractive option for many buildings despite these challenges.

Solar costs in Mumbai

₹60,000 - ₹85,000 per kW

highest in India due to urban premium and installation complexity

Why Mumbai costs more:

  • Limited space increases logistics complexity
  • High-rise building installation challenges
  • Premium labor and material costs
  • Structural reinforcement requirements
  • Crane and special equipment rentals

Despite the high costs, Mumbai's electricity tariffs (among India's highest) and space constraints create a compelling case for solar. Many buildings find that solar is the only viable option to reduce electricity costs and ensure power reliability in the face of growing demand.

Approvals & timeline in Mumbai

Electricity Providers

BEST, TATA Power, MSEDCL (depending on location)

Typical Timeline

30-50 days from application to commissioning

Cooperative societies may need 4-6 weeks additional approval

Mumbai's unique approval challenges:

  • Multiple DISCOMs serving different areas
  • Cooperative society approvals mandatory for apartments
  • Municipal Corporation approvals for structural changes
  • Fire safety and building code compliance
  • Clearance from aviation authority for high-rise buildings

The approval process in Mumbai is more complex than other cities due to the density of development and multiple regulatory bodies involved. However, experienced installers in Mumbai are familiar with these requirements and can navigate them effectively.

Societies and commercial buildings with shared infrastructure

Mumbai's extreme space constraints mean solar adoption patterns differ from other cities. Individual homes and small apartments often install only 1-3 kW systems (what space allows), while the most significant adoption happens in housing societies and commercial buildings with shared roof space.
