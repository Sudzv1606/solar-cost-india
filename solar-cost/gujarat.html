Solar Installation Cost in Gujarat (2025)

Gujarat stands out as India's most cost-effective state for rooftop solar installations, with some of the lowest installation costs in the country combined with excellent solar irradiation. This page explains the unique advantages of installing solar in Gujarat.

Gujarat offers the most competitive solar installation costs in India, driven by:

  • State's early adoption of solar policies
  • Excellent logistics and manufacturing infrastructure
  • High competition among installers
  • Simplified digital application processes

The state's proactive approach to renewable energy has created a mature ecosystem that benefits consumers through competitive pricing and streamlined processes. For exact estimates based on your specific location in Gujarat, use the calculator.

Electricity tariffs & impact on savings

Gujarat's residential electricity tariffs are structured to make solar financially attractive:

  • Moderate base tariffs with clear tier structure
  • Net metering benefits fully realized through monthly settlement
  • Time-of-day pricing in urban areas increases solar value

While Gujarat's tariffs may not be the highest in India, the extremely low installation costs combined with excellent solar irradiation create one of the best value propositions for rooftop solar in the country.

DISCOM process

Four distribution companies cover Gujarat:

  • PGVCL - Paschim Gujarat Vij Company Limited
  • DGVCL - Dakshin Gujarat Vij Company Limited
  • UGVCL - Uttar Gujarat Vij Company Limited
  • MGVCL - Madhya Gujarat Vij Company Limited

Gujarat has pioneered digital application processes with online net metering applications, making it one of the most efficient states for solar approvals.

Is solar worth it in Gujarat?

Solar is highly recommended when:

  • Any residential property with own roof
  • Monthly electricity bills of ₹2,000 or more
  • Properties with high daytime electricity usage

Consider carefully when:

  • Properties with heavy shading or limited roof space
  • Areas with frequent power cuts (need battery backup)
  • Very low monthly consumption (<₹1,000)< /li>

Gujarat's excellent solar irradiation (highest in India), combined with the lowest installation costs and efficient processes, makes it arguably the best state in India for rooftop solar adoption. The state government's continued support for solar ensures a stable policy environment for long-term investments.
