Solar Installation Cost in Bangalore (2025)

Bangalore stands out as one of India's most favorable cities for solar adoption, combining high electricity tariffs with a tech-savvy population that's increasingly adopting rooftop solar. The city's excellent solar potential and supportive policies make it a prime market for residential solar installations.

Solar costs in Bangalore

₹50,000 - ₹72,000 per kW

including panels, inverter, and installation

What makes Bangalore's costs unique:

  • High competition among installers
  • Easy access to quality equipment
  • Modern installation techniques
  • BESCOM's streamlined processes

While Bangalore's costs are slightly higher than Karnataka's average due to urban premiums, the excellent return on investment makes it one of India's most attractive cities for solar. The city's educated homeowners typically understand the long-term benefits and are willing to invest in quality systems.

Approvals & timeline in Bangalore

Electricity Provider

BESCOM (Bangalore Electricity Supply Company Limited)

Typical Timeline

25-40 days from application to commissioning

Apartments may require additional 2-4 weeks for society approvals

Bangalore's process advantages:

  • Digital application portal for faster processing
  • Clear documentation requirements
  • Transparent net metering policy
  • Professional installer ecosystem

BESCOM has implemented several initiatives to streamline solar approvals, making Bangalore one of the easier cities for solar installation in India.
