Solar Installation Cost in Mumbai (2025)

Higher costs, space constraints, but better savings • Complete guide for Mumbai homes

Current Solar Prices in Mumbai

Market Pricing (Updated January 2025)

₹60,000 - ₹85,000 per kW

Includes: Panels, inverter, mounting structure, installation

Excludes: BEST/TATA Power approval fees, net meter cost

20-30% higher than Pune due to logistics, labor costs, and high-rise installation challenges

System Size Recommendations for Mumbai Homes

1. Small Apartment (Monthly Bill: ₹2,000 - ₹4,000)

  • System Size: 2-3 kW
  • Total Cost: ₹1.2 - ₹2.5 lakh
  • Subsidy: ₹78,000 (for up to 2 kW) or ₹1.17 lakh (for 3 kW)
  • Net Cost: ₹43,000 - ₹1.73 lakh
  • Payback: 4-5 years

2. Bungalow/Row House (Monthly Bill: ₹4,000 - ₹8,000)

  • System Size: 4-6 kW
  • Total Cost: ₹2.4 - ₹5.1 lakh
  • Subsidy: ₹1.17 lakh (capped at 3 kW)
  • Net Cost: ₹1.23 - ₹3.93 lakh
  • Payback: 5-6 years

Mumbai-Specific Challenges

Space Constraints

  • Most apartments: 500-800 sq ft usable roof area
  • Maximum system: 3-4 kW for typical apartments
  • Society permission required for terrace installation

Higher Installation Costs

  • High-rise installation requires special equipment
  • Parking and material handling challenges
  • Labor costs 30% higher than Pune

Coastal Environment

  • Higher humidity and salt air corrosion risk
  • Need for marine-grade components
  • Frequent cleaning required (every month in monsoon)

Approval Process in Mumbai

BEST (Brihanmumbai Electric Supply)

Step 1: Application (5-7 days)

Submit at BEST office with NOC from society

Step 2: Technical Approval (15-20 days)

Structural audit and feasibility check

Step 3: Installation (7-10 days)

Longer due to high-rise logistics

Step 4: Net Meter (20-30 days)

Processing delays common

Total Timeline: 50-70 days (longer than Pune)

TATA Power Areas

Similar process but with slightly faster approval (40-60 days)

Different subsidy portal - check TATA Power website

Why Mumbai Still Makes Sense

  • High Electricity Rates: ₹8-10/unit (highest in Maharashtra)
  • Better Solar Generation: 5.3 kWh/m²/day
  • Frequent Power Cuts: Backup during outages
  • Property Value: Solar increases property premium
  • Environmental Impact: Reduce carbon footprint significantly

Special Considerations for Mumbai

Building Norms

  • 70% open space must be maintained ( BMC rules)
  • Shadow study mandatory for high-rises
  • Wind load calculations for coastal areas

Maintenance

  • Monthly cleaning during monsoon season
  • Annual corrosion inspection
  • Inverter placement must be protected from humidity

Mumbai Solar Reality Check

Don't install solar if: Your bill is under ₹2,000/month or you have less than 400 sq ft shadow-free roof area

Perfect for: Bungalows in Bandra, Juhu; Row houses in Andheri; Large terraces in Goregaon
