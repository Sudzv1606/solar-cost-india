When Solar Makes Perfect Sense

✅ Monthly bill above ₹3,000

Higher bills mean faster payback. At ₹5,000/month, solar pays back in 3.5 years.

✅ Own your house with shadow-free roof

Essential for eligibility and maximum efficiency. Need minimum 400 sq ft area.

✅ Plan to stay in the same house 5+ years

Solar needs 4-5 years to pay back. Perfect if you're not moving soon.

✅ Area has frequent power cuts

Solar provides reliable backup. Additional battery investment of ₹1-2 lakh recommended.

When Grid Electricity Might Be Better

❌ Monthly bill below ₹1,500

Payback period exceeds 7 years. Not financially viable.

❌ Living in rented apartment

Not eligible for subsidy. Can't recover investment when moving.

❌ Heavily shaded roof

Shadow reduces output by 30-50%. Not worth the investment.

❌ Planning to sell/rent property in 2-3 years

Won't recover investment before moving.

Decision Scenarios

Scenario 1: Pune Family with ₹5,000 monthly bill

With Solar:

  • System: 4 kW
  • Cost: ₹2.6 lakh
  • Subsidy: ₹1.17 lakh
  • Net cost: ₹1.43 lakh
  • Monthly saving: ₹3,500
  • Payback: 3.4 years
  • 25-year savings: ₹9 lakh

Without Solar:

  • 25-year cost: ₹22.5 lakh
  • Price increases every year
  • Dependent on grid
  • No power backup

Recommendation: Go Solar

Scenario 2: Mumbai Apartment with ₹2,000 monthly bill

With Solar:

  • System: 1.5 kW
  • Cost: ₹1.1 lakh
  • Subsidy: Not applicable
  • Net cost: ₹1.1 lakh
  • Monthly saving: ₹1,200
  • Payback: 7.6 years
  • 25-year savings: ₹2.5 lakh

Without Solar:

  • 25-year cost: ₹9 lakh
  • Highest tariffs in Maharashtra
  • No investment required

Recommendation: Stick to Grid (unless you have additional reasons for energy independence)
