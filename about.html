About Solar Cost India

Helping homeowners make confident solar decisions — before talking to installers

Our Mission

Solar decisions shouldn't be confusing. Solar Cost India provides honest, location-specific cost estimates to help Indian homeowners understand their solar options without installer bias.

Why We Exist

Most homeowners interested in solar face three problems:

  • Confusing Pricing: Installer quotes vary wildly (₹45k to ₹85k per kW)
  • Complex Subsidies: Central + state programs with unclear eligibility
  • Information Bias: Most content is created by installers trying to sell

We solve this by providing independent, data-driven estimates based on MNRE benchmarks and real market research.

How We're Different

🎯 Location-Specific

Not national averages. Real pricing for your state, city, and DISCOM.

📊 Data-Driven

Based on MNRE benchmarks, DISCOM tariffs, and verified installer quotes.

✓ Honest

We show ranges, not exact numbers. We admit uncertainty. No false promises.

🔒 Independent

Not owned by installers. Not a lead-selling platform. Information-first.

Our Data Sources

  • MNRE: Ministry of New and Renewable Energy benchmark pricing and subsidy rules
  • DISCOMs: State electricity board tariff structures and net metering policies
  • Market Research: Real quotes from 50+ empanelled installers across cities
  • Location Intelligence: City-specific factors (accessibility, labor costs, competition)

What We Don't Do

To maintain trust and accuracy, we deliberately avoid:

  • ❌ Guaranteeing exact prices (only installers can do that after site survey)
  • ❌ Promising subsidy approval (depends on government verification)
  • ❌ Selling leads before showing estimates (estimates are always free)
  • ❌ Favoring specific installers (all connections are unbiased)

Coverage

Currently serving:

  • 4 States: Maharashtra, Karnataka, Gujarat, Delhi NCR
  • 10+ Cities: Including Pune, Mumbai, Bangalore, Nagpur, and more
  • Growing: Adding new locations based on demand

Our Commitment

We pledge to:

  • ✓ Keep estimates free forever
  • ✓ Update data quarterly (MNRE + market research)
  • ✓ Disclose all assumptions and limitations
  • ✓ Never sell your data to third parties
  • ✓ Remain installer-independent
